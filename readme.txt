Lastmal helps businesses to complete shipping and delivery activities simply and efficiently anywhere within West Africa with one integration.

== Robust & Efficient Woocommerce Shipping Integration ==

Lastmal Shipping provides your Woocommerce store with a robust and efficient shipping method. It takes just 5 minutes to integrate this plugin.

Your clients will see the Lastmal shipping option at checkout with real-time delivery rates. Shipping rates are calculated automatically online due to our robust API connection. 

You also get the option to customize the service type offerings (i.e same-day, next-day, or 3-day deliveries, etc.) for your store.

== Features ==

* Automatic shipping cost calculated with Lastmal live rates
* Customize delivery service options
* Help reduce cart abandonment
* Handle digital payment for your store
* Very minimal human intervention to complete shipping
* Automatic sync with your Lastmal Distribution Integration Platform. 
* [Get Started](https://apps.lastmal.com/get-started) for free!

Note: You will need a [Lastmal API Key](https://app.lastmal.com/login) to use the plugin.
